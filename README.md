## 📌project overview
AI-Based Health Advisory System Using Machine Learning and Deep Learning** is an intelligent healthcare prediction and advisory solution designed to support **early prediction of Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD)** and assist healthcare decision-making through predictive analytics and personalized healthcare recommendations.

Healthcare systems often face challenges in **early disease detection, patient monitoring, and timely medical intervention**, which can lead to delayed treatments and increased health risks. This project aims to address these challenges by leveraging **Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and Deep Learning (DL)** technologies to provide intelligent healthcare insights and predictive decision support.

The system is developed to analyze **patient medical records, laboratory reports, demographic details, and clinical health parameters** in order to predict the risk of **Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD)** and estimate the possibility of **dialysis initiation at an early stage**. By processing medical information such as **serum creatinine, eGFR, blood urea nitrogen (BUN), patient history, laboratory test reports, and clinical indicators**, the system helps identify health risks before the condition becomes severe.

To achieve this, the project integrates **data preprocessing, feature engineering, predictive modeling, healthcare analytics, and Deep Learning-based prediction techniques** to analyze hidden patterns in healthcare datasets and improve prediction accuracy. Advanced Machine Learning and Deep Learning algorithms are applied to generate reliable predictive insights for disease progression and healthcare monitoring.

A **web-based healthcare application** was also developed to provide a user-friendly platform where users can enter patient medical information, monitor prediction results, and receive **personalized healthcare recommendations** based on predictive analysis. The system is designed to improve accessibility, prediction accuracy, and healthcare decision-making while supporting preventive medical care.

The project also includes an intelligent **health advisory mechanism** that supports healthcare professionals and users by providing **risk prediction, patient monitoring insights, and personalized healthcare guidance** based on historical and real-time patient data. Unlike traditional methods that rely on isolated medical indicators, this system uses a **data-driven healthcare personalization approach** to improve prediction accuracy and support informed treatment planning.

This solution focuses on building a **smart, reliable, scalable, and AI-powered healthcare advisory system** that can improve **early CKD detection, dialysis prediction, preventive healthcare, patient monitoring, and medical decision-making**. By combining **Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, predictive analytics, healthcare data processing, and web technologies**, the project demonstrates how modern technology can solve real-world healthcare challenges effectively.
